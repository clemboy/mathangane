About me


My name is Nosimpiwe Qonya.

I was born and bred in Dimbaze, Eastern Cape and im currently residing
in Pretoria, Gauteng.

Im the founder and Ceo of Chef Mathangane Catering Service. I started
Chef Mathangane because i enjoy cooking and serving. I decided to
turn my passion into a business opportunity.

Chef Mathangane is also a private chef who offers house calls, picnic setup
brunch and tailor made menu. I specialise in fine dining and pastry at
affordable prices.

I also sell organic infused infused olive oil that comes in different flavours.
